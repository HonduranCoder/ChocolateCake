For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir tro dissolve. Half all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chunky-jammy mixture. Add saly with one or two stirs, se aside and let cool completely.

Ice Cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and the millk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge amd wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturer instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding marscarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to process for +/-10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a Pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.

  1. 1 1/2 cups milk
  2. 1/2 cup marscarpone
  3. 1/2 tsp pink salt
  4. 1lb mission figs
  5. 1/2 cup brown sugar
  6. 2-4 tbsp water
  1. 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  2. 1/3 granulated sugar
  3. 2 egg yolks
  4. 1 lemon, juiced
  5. 2 tbsp butter
  6. 1 cup honey roasted pecans, rooughly chopped
